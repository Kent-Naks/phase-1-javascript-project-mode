Welcome to Our Service

The pressing question remains: where do you truly begin when a loved one goes missing? Is the first step reporting it to your nearest police station? Should you then visit every other police station in your county? Which hospital do you start with, and which one do you end with? At what point do you finally decide to visit the morgues within your county?

Why endure all this? At MSP, we believe there is a 95% chance that someone out there knows your loved one's whereabouts and current condition.

In alignment with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) under the 2030 Global UN Agenda, we strive to provide a tool for social justice, focusing on good health and well-being, with an emphasis on mental health and peace of mind. Additionally, we advocate for peace, justice, and strong institutions. For we know, justice delayed is justice denied.

About Us

We are here to reunite families with those they have lost and to reduce the burden of body identification for the police force and morgues. As well as to reduce the strain of resources on public and private hospitals over unclaimed people/bodies.
